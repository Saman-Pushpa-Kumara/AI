Info & Policy

📜 Game Rules
• Movement: Pieces move diagonally forward.
• Jumping: You can jump over opponent pieces to capture them. Backward jumping is allowed!
• Multi-Jump: If another jump is available after a capture, you must take it.
• King (දාම්): Reaching the opposite end crowns your piece. Kings can move diagonally both forward and backward.
🔒 Privacy Policy
• No Data Collection: This game does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
• Offline Play: The game functions 100% offline once loaded or installed via PWA.
• Local Storage: Game state may be stored locally on your device to allow resuming.
💡 App Info
• Version: 2.0.1 (Pro 3D)
• Technology: HTML5, CSS3 Glassmorphism, Vanilla JS, Service Workers.
• Designed for optimal mobile performance and minimal battery usage.
